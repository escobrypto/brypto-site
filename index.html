Rules
No Financial Advice. All discussions are for educational and informational purposes only. Nothing shared here constitutes financial, legal, or investment advice.

No Scams, Shilling, or Market Manipulation. Scams, pump-and-dumps, coordinated shilling, or promotion of fraudulent or unsolicited projects are strictly prohibited.

No Advertising, Links, or Promotion. Posting or sharing links of any kind is not allowed unless explicitly approved by the moderation team. This includes websites, social media, Discord/Telegram invites, referral links, affiliate links, and tracking links.

No Circumventing Rules or Moderation. Any attempt to bypass server rules or moderation actions is strictly prohibited. This includes evading filters, re-posting removed content, using alternate accounts, or encouraging others to violate rules.

No DM Advertising or Cold Outreach. Do not privately message members to promote anything. Unsolicited DMs should be reported to the moderation team.

DYOR. Always verify information independently. Do not blindly trust any post, chart, or user, including moderators or admins.

Be Respectful. Harassment, hate speech, threats, personal attacks, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated. Disagreements are expected. Hostility is not.

No Spam. Spamming messages, reactions, tags, or images is not allowed.

Use the Correct Channels. Keep discussions in their appropriate channels.

No Misinformation. Do not spread unverified rumors, false claims, or fear-mongering.

Content Guidelines. NSFW content, explicit material, or inappropriate imagery is not allowed.

Follow Moderator Instructions. Moderators maintain order. Their guidance should be respected.

Username & Profile Standards. Offensive usernames, avatars, or impersonation of others are not allowed.

Report Suspicious Activity. If you see behavior that violates these rules or appears suspicious, report it immediately.
